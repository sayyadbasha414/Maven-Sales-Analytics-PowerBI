📊 Maven Market Analytics Dashboard
Overview

The Maven Market Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, transactions, returns, and regional business performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights that help stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Business Requirements

The objective of this project is to:
Track sales and profit performance across different regions.
Monitor monthly transactions and product returns.
Analyze product-level profitability and performance.
Compare actual revenue against business targets.
Identify high-performing markets and business opportunities.
Provide an easy-to-use dashboard for management reporting.
